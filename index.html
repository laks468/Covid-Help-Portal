take precautions against covid-19

things you should DO

wash your hand

Make sure to wash hands after you blow your nose, when you leave and return to your home, before preparing or eating food.

always wear a mask

Wear two-layer mask. Clean your hands before putting on and taking off a mask.

stay home during fever

Cover your coughs and sneezes with a bent elbow or tissue, throwing used tissues into a closed bin right away.

things you should NOT DO

avoid close contact with animals

Prefer indoor locations and avoid prolonged contact with others. Animals may also serve as medium of spreading virus.

avoid close contact with peoples

Spend more time indoors.Keep your distance. Stay at least 1 metre away from others.

avoid crowded area

Avoid crowded places, poorly ventilated, indoor locations and avoid prolonged contact with others.
